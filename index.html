Virat Kohli Continues to Dominate the ICC T20 World Cup 2025

Indian cricket superstar Virat Kohli has once again captured global attention with his record-breaking feats in the ICC T20 World Cup 2025. Known for his passion, aggression, and reliability under pressure, Kohli’s latest performances prove why he’s one of the all-time greats in international cricket. His ability to consistently deliver under pressure has made him a cornerstone of India’s batting lineup and a fan favorite worldwide.

13,000 T20I Runs: A Monumental Milestone

During the Super 8 clash against Australia, Virat Kohli crossed the 13,000-run mark in T20 internationals, an achievement never before reached in the format. This historic milestone not only highlights his longevity but also his unmatched consistency in the shortest format of the game. Kohli’s ability to adapt to different conditions and bowlers has been key to this success.

This landmark run tally cements his legacy as the most consistent and prolific T20I batsman in cricket history. His technique, combined with an aggressive mindset, allows him to dominate bowling attacks and anchor innings when needed.

  • Total T20I Runs: 13,025+
  • Strike Rate in World Cup 2025: 142.50
  • Average in Tournament: 54.6

Match-Winning Knock Against Australia

In one of the most crucial matches of the tournament, Kohli delivered a masterclass innings against Australia in the Super 8s. His controlled aggression and calculated stroke play helped India chase down a competitive total with ease. Kohli’s innings was a perfect blend of patience and power, showcasing his ability to pace an innings according to the match situation.

His 78* off 52 balls was studded with 9 fours and 2 sixes, demonstrating his range of shots and ability to find gaps consistently. This knock not only helped India secure a vital win but also boosted the team’s morale heading into the later stages of the tournament.

Match Highlights – India vs Australia (Super 8)

  • Virat Kohli’s Score: 78* (52 balls)
  • Boundaries: 9 fours, 2 sixes
  • Result: India won by 5 wickets
  • Award: Player of the Match

Back-to-Back Player of the Match Awards

Kohli was also named Player of the Match in India’s previous fixture against South Africa, where he scored a quick-fire 64 runs off 38 balls. This innings was a display of his aggressive intent and ability to accelerate the scoring rate when the team needed it most.

With two consecutive Player of the Match awards, Kohli now leads the MVP race of the ICC T20 World Cup 2025. His consistent match-winning performances have made him the backbone of India’s batting lineup and a nightmare for opposing bowlers.
